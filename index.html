ABOUT US

It’s been an exciting journey this far. Alongside a strong presence in manufacturing durable safety footwear, personal protective equipment (PPE) workwear, leather gloves, safety helmets, and army garments. We’ve developed our edge in crafting leather and ancillary products. We’ve also forayed into home care and medical products, real estate, infrastructure and other emerging opportunities. Yet when we look ahead, we believe the best is yet to come.
